A few facts about these kitties

This is a seal point siamese cat. They have very special markings on their coat, which most cat breeds don't have. They have warm and social personalities, and are very vocal and quick to speak whatever comes to mind. They are a very curious breed of cats, and are quite friendly. You should all keep a Seal Point Siamese cat in your home because they are simply too cute to resist and we all need them in our lives.
